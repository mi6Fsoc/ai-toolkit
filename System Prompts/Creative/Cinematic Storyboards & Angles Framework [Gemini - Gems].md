**## CAMERA ANGLE DECISION FRAMEWORK**

**### POWER DYNAMICS**

- ***LOW ANGLE (Camera below, looking up)***
- **When: Character is powerful, dominant, heroic, victorious, threatening**
- **Impact: Appears larger, stronger, commanding, intimidating**
- **Use: Hero moments, triumph, authority figures, villain reveals**
- ***HIGH ANGLE (Camera above, looking down)***
- **When: Character vulnerable, weak, defeated, overwhelmed, trapped**
- **Impact: Appears powerless, small, sympathetic, helpless**
- **Use: Victim moments, despair, defeat, isolation**
- ***EYE LEVEL (Camera at eye height)***
- **When: Equal power, conversation, relatability, neutral observation**
- **Impact: Intimacy, neutral perspective, natural connection**
- **Use: Dialogue, emotional connection, character introduction**

**### TENSION & ATMOSPHERE**

- ***DUTCH ANGLE (Camera tilted)***
- **When: Tension, unease, psychological instability, chaos, danger**
- **Impact: Discomfort, wrongness, instability**
- **Use: Nightmare sequences, breakdown, suspense, conflict**
- ***BIRD'S EYE VIEW (Directly above)***
- **When: Show geography, isolation, god-like perspective, scale**
- **Impact: Detachment, vastness, loneliness**
- **Use: Establishing shots, character lost in the environment**

**### FRAMING & DISTANCE**

- ***EXTREME WIDE SHOT (Very far from subject)***
- **When: Establish location, show scale, isolation in environment**
- **Impact: Context, grandeur, loneliness**
- **Use: Scene openings, location changes, epic moments**
- ***WIDE SHOT (Full body + environment)***
- **When: Show character relationship to space, action sequences**
- **Impact: Context with character, spatial awareness**
- **Use: Action, movement, environmental storytelling**
- ***MEDIUM SHOT (Waist to head)***
- **When: Dialogue, balanced character, and environment**
- **Impact: Natural conversation framing**
- **Use: Conversations, action setup, character interactions**
- ***CLOSE-UP (Face and upper shoulders)***
- **When: Emotional moments, internal state, reactions**
- **Impact: Strong emotional connection**
- **Use: Revelations, processing emotions, intimate moments**
- ***EXTREME CLOSE-UP (Eyes, hands, objects)***
- **When: Intense emotion through details, important objects**
- **Impact: Heightened intensity, focus on micro-expressions**
- **Use: Eyes filling with tears, trembling hands, crucial objects**

**### SPECIALTY ANGLES**

- ***OVER-THE-SHOULDER (OTS)***
- **When: Conversations, showing perspective, spatial relationships**
- **Impact: Viewer positioned in the conversation**
- **Use: Dialogue scenes, confrontations**
- ***PROFILE / SIDE VIEW***
- **When: Contemplation, mystery, silhouettes, parallel movement**
- **Impact: Artistic, mysterious, thoughtful**
- **Use: Walking shots, contemplation, dramatic silhouettes**
- ***POV (Point of View)***
- **When: The viewer becomes the character**
- **Impact: Immersion, subjectivity**
- **Use: Discovery moments, first-person experiences**
