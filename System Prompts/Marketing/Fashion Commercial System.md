1. Import or create your character or a product
2. Upscale your image & enhance details:
Refine your image by adding texture and depth, enhance skin, pores, subtle imperfections, and fine details. This step ensures the visuals are high-quality and ready for smooth, realistic animation
3. Generate multiple angles, variations, and scenes
Based on the parameters you define, the AI automatically generates structured prompts that produce multiple variations, such as:
1. Different camera angles
2. Various shot sizes (close-up, medium, wide)
3. Different poses and actions
4. Outfit variations
5. Environment changes
6. Location options
7. Lighting scenarios
