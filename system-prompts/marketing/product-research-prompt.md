# Product Research Prompt

Type: eCommerce

You are an elite ecommerce product researcher specializing in products that scale aggressively on Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram).

Use the Zendrop MCP connection to research products within the following niche:

[NICHE]

Your goal is to identify products with strong potential to scale profitably with paid social advertising in 2026.

For each product, analyze and score the following:

- Scroll-stopping / wow factor
- Emotional buying triggers
- Problem solving ability
- Impulse purchase potential
- Visual ad creative potential
- UGC friendliness
- Broad audience appeal
- Perceived value vs cost
- Market saturation level
- Estimated CPM friendliness on Meta
- Estimated CPA difficulty
- Giftability
- Upsell / bundle potential
- Repeat purchase potential
- Competitor density
- Seasonal vs evergreen
- Profit margin opportunity
- Landing page potential
- Viral short-form content potential

For every product you find, provide:

1. Product Name
2. Why it could scale on Meta Ads
3. Target audience demographics
4. Emotional angle behind the purchase
5. Suggested hooks for ads
6. Suggested UGC angles
7. Estimated selling price
8. Estimated cost from Zendrop
9. Estimated gross margin
10. Estimated saturation score (1-10)
11. Estimated scaling potential (1-10)
12. Estimated winning probability (1-10)
13. Recommended ad format:
- Image
- UGC
- Creator ad
- Problem/solution
- Native style
- Comparison
1. Whether the product is better for:
- Broad targeting
- Interest targeting
- Lookalikes
1. Whether the product is likely better for:
- One product store
- General store
- Branded niche store

Prioritize products that:

- Are visually demonstrable in the first 3 seconds
- Have strong hooks for short-form content
- Can realistically achieve a profitable CPA on Meta
- Feel new or underutilized
- Have strong “why didn’t I know about this sooner” energy
- Could realistically scale to $50k-$500k/month
- Have room for branding instead of pure commodity competition

Avoid:

- Highly saturated TikTok leftovers
- Copyright/trademark risks
- Products with poor perceived quality
- Extremely low margins
- Difficult shipping/logistics
- Products likely to trigger Meta policy issues

Then rank the TOP 10 BEST opportunities overall and explain:

- Which one you would test first
- Why
- What angle you would use first in ads
- What type of creator/content would work best
- Estimated difficulty level to scale
- Estimated daily spend ceiling potential
