### 2. Hook & first 3 seconds

- **Opening frame:** What’s the *very first* thing you see?
- **Hook type:**
    - **Pattern interrupt:** weird visual, unexpected line, jump cut, etc.
    - **Tension question:** “What happens if…”, “I tried X for 30 days…”
    - **Relatable moment:** everyday pain, meme, POV shot
- **Hook line (if any):** Write it down verbatim.
- **Why it stops the scroll:** Describe in 1–2 sentences.

---

### 3. Emotional engine

- **Primary emotion:** (joy, awe, tension, FOMO, pride, belonging, etc.)
- **Secondary emotion:** (often contrast—tension → relief, boredom → excitement)

- **Moment of emotional release:** Exact second/frame where you *feel* something shift.

### 4. Story structure & pacing

- **Story spine (even if short):**

    - **Conflict/tension:** What’s the problem or curiosity?
    - **Resolution:** How does it resolve or transform?
- **Pacing style:**
    - **Fast cuts / meme-y / chaotic**
    - **Steady / cinematic / slow build**

### 5. Visual language

- **Color palette:** (warm, neon, muted, monochrome, etc. + what it signals)
- **Camera style:** (POV, handheld, tripod, drone, macro, etc.)
- **Framing & composition:** (centered, rule of thirds, close-ups, negative space)
- **Texture & finish:** (glossy, gritty, analog, hyperreal, etc.)

---

### 6. Sound & rhythm

- **Music:** (genre, tempo, mood; existing track vs original)
- **Voiceover:** (tone: casual, authoritative, whisper, comedic, etc.)
- **Key line(s):** Write down exact phrases that stick.
- **Sound effects / ASMR moments:**
- **Sync with visuals:** Do cuts land on beats? Any “drop” moment?

---

### 7. Psychological levers

- **Main persuasion angle:**
    - **Social proof:** “everyone uses this”
    - **Authority:** expert/celebrity/brand legacy
    - **Scarcity:** limited time/slots
    - **Novelty:** “never seen before”
    - **Relatability:** “this is so me”
    - **Identity:** “people like us do X”
- **Objection handling:** How do they neutralize doubt (price, effort, trust)?
- **Call to action:** Exact CTA + how early it appears.

---

### 8. Platform-native mechanics

- **Format:** (vertical, square, 16:9)
- **Native behaviors used:**
    - Trends, stitches, duets, memes, green screen, subtitles, etc.
- **Retention tricks:**
    - Tease early, reveal late
    - Visual puzzles
    - Fast pattern changes
- **Shareability hooks:**
    - “You have to see this”
    - “Tag someone who…”
    - Challenge/participation angle

---

### 9. Brand integration

- **Brand reveal timing:** (early, mid, late)
- **Subtle vs heavy branding:** Describe.
- **Tone of voice:** (playful, premium, rebellious, wholesome, etc.)
- **Does branding break immersion or feel native?** Why?

- **Influencers/UGC involved?**
- **Trend hijack or original format?**
- **How people would caption it when sharing:** Write 2–3 likely captions.
