# AI UGC / AI Influencer Video Style Guidelines

### “Natural, Organic, iPhone-Style Content” Prompt Add-On

Copy/paste these into your avatar prompts, video prompts, script instructions, or character sheets whenever you want content to feel authentic instead of cinematic.

---

## Core Style Direction

* Shot like authentic user-generated content (UGC)
* Looks filmed casually on an iPhone
* Natural and organic social media aesthetic
* Imperfect realism over polished cinematics
* Prioritize authenticity and relatability
* Feels like TikTok/Reels/Instagram Story content
* Creator/influencer style rather than commercial production
* Documentary realism
* Casual social-first framing
* Raw but clean
* Human imperfections preserved
* Everyday environment and lighting
* Native social media energy

---

## Camera & Motion Rules

* Handheld iPhone-style camera movement
* Subtle natural hand movement
* Steady cam feel but not robotic stabilization
* No cinematic camera movements
* No dramatic pans
* No dramatic tracking shots
* No crane shots
* No dolly shots
* No cinematic orbit shots
* No aggressive motion
* No artificial camera shake
* No action-movie movement
* Minimal movement overall
* Mostly static framing
* Casual selfie framing
* Slight framing imperfections allowed
* Natural body movement
* Eye-level perspective
* Human-operated feel

---

## Zoom & Lens Rules

* No zoom-ins
* No dramatic zoom-outs
* No crash zooms
* No cinematic lens compression
* No anamorphic lens look
* No shallow depth-of-field cinematography
* Mostly everything in focus
* Smartphone lens appearance
* Slight wide-angle phone-camera look
* Digital phone-camera realism
* Natural perspective distortion acceptable

---

## Lighting Guidelines

* Natural lighting preferred
* Soft daylight
* Window lighting
* Indoor ambient lighting
* Practical household lighting
* Slight exposure imperfections allowed
* No dramatic studio lighting
* No cinematic rim lighting
* No colored film lighting
* No over-produced lighting setups
* Realistic shadows
* Slight inconsistency in lighting is acceptable
* Casual home/apartment/office lighting

---

## Video Quality & Texture

* iPhone camera quality
* Slight smartphone compression feel
* Realistic skin texture
* Preserve natural imperfections
* No hyper-detailed CGI skin
* No overly sharpened footage
* No beauty-filter appearance
* No glossy commercial polish
* Slight motion blur acceptable
* Slight grain acceptable
* Organic social-media rendering
* Natural dynamic range
* Avoid HDR cinematic grading

---

## Framing & Composition

* Social-media-first framing
* Vertical video composition
* TikTok/Reels framing
* Casual composition
* Slightly imperfect framing
* Headroom inconsistencies acceptable
* Subject not always perfectly centered
* Natural selfie-camera angles
* Close conversational framing
* Influencer talking-head framing
* Realistic room spacing/backgrounds
* Everyday environments

---

## Acting & Performance Style

* Conversational delivery
* Natural pauses
* Slight filler words acceptable
* Authentic facial expressions
* Relatable energy
* Subtle imperfections in speech
* Human pacing
* Casual gestures
* Influencer-style delivery
* Direct-to-camera speaking
* Genuine reactions
* Slight awkwardness acceptable
* Avoid polished commercial acting
* Avoid theatrical performance
* Understated emotion

---

## Editing Style

* Minimal editing
* No cinematic transitions
* No flashy effects
* No movie-style cuts
* Simple jump cuts acceptable
* TikTok/Reels pacing
* Fast but natural pacing
* Native platform editing style
* No excessive motion graphics
* Minimal captions
* Organic social-media editing
* Avoid over-produced sound design
* Avoid cinematic sound effects

---

## Audio Style

* Natural voice tone
* Slight room ambience acceptable
* Realistic phone microphone feel
* No overly polished studio audio
* Clean but casual audio
* Authentic environmental sound
* Natural speech dynamics
* No dramatic cinematic music
* Soft background ambience only if needed

---

## Environment Guidelines

* Realistic apartment/home/office settings
* Lived-in environments
* Everyday backgrounds
* Slight background clutter acceptable
* Natural props
