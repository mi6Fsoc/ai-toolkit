# Framework: AI-Generated Marketing Visuals (Claude)

## **Core Prompt Formula**

Use this 4-component structure for all image prompts:

**"A [IMAGE TYPE] of [SUBJECT] in [BACKGROUND SETTING], [STYLE]"**

### **Components:**

- **Image Type**: photo, illustration, product shot, diagram, etc.
- **Subject**: primary focus (person, product, scene, object)
- **Background Setting**: environment or context
- **Style**: visual style, mood, lighting, artistic direction

## **Prompt Construction Rules**

### **1. Specificity Requirements**

- Use exact descriptions over generic terms
- Include colors, textures, lighting details
- Specify composition (close-up vs. wide shot, centered vs. off-center)
- For text overlay needs: mention "negative space on [left/right/top/bottom]"

### **2. Style Modifiers**

Choose from these proven descriptors:

- **Quality**: "hyper-realistic", "ultra-detailed", "4K photograph", "high resolution"
- **Lighting**: "soft studio lighting", "natural lighting", "cinematic lighting"
- **Composition**: "centered", "rule of thirds", "minimalist composition"
- **Artistic**: "flat illustration", "vintage film style", "isometric 3D render"

### **3. Brand Alignment Integration**

Always include:

- Brand color schemes (use hex codes when specific)
- Brand style adjectives ("playful, modern" or "luxurious, sleek")
- Consistent style descriptors across campaign assets
- Brand elements context when relevant

## **Marketing Use Case Templates**

### **Blog Content**

**Featured Image**: "A [illustration/photo] for a post about [blog topic] in [desired style], set in [background scene], with space for title overlay"

**Explainer Graphic**: "An illustration explaining [concept] featuring [key elements] in [minimalist/detailed style], against [clean background]"

### **Digital Advertising**

**Product Ad**: "A photo of [product] on [surface] in [studio setting], [lighting style], centered with negative space for text"

**Seasonal Campaign**: "A product photo of [product] with [holiday/seasonal decor] in [style], evoking [specific mood/atmosphere]"

**Comparison Shot**: "Side-by-side photo of [product A] and [product B] on [background], highlighting [key difference], in [clean/professional style]"

### **Social Media**

**Product Promo**: "A product photo of [product] against [background] in [Instagram-friendly style], with space on [side] for text overlay"

**Lifestyle Post**: "A lifestyle photo of [target persona] using [product] in [relatable setting], [warm/energetic style]"

**Contest/Giveaway**: "An arranged photo of [prize items] on [surface] with [brand colors], in [exciting/premium style]"

### **E-commerce/Product Marketing**

**Flat Lay**: "A product flat lay of [products] arranged on [textured surface] with [props], in [clean/artistic style], top-down view"

**Product in Use**: "[Product] being used by [target persona] in [realistic setting], highlighting [key benefit/feature], [natural lighting]"

**Detail Shot**: "A close-up macro photo of [product detail/texture] with [dramatic/soft lighting], showing [specific feature]"

### **Website Graphics**

**Hero Banner**: "A [photo/illustration] showcasing [main subject] in [aspirational background], [brand-aligned style], optimized for web banner format"

**Landing Page**: "An illustration of [service/concept] with [relevant icons/elements] in [modern/professional style], suitable for conversion-focused page"

### **Email Marketing**

**Header Image**: "An email header featuring [product/theme] in [brand colors] with [style], designed for [campaign type]"

**Product Teaser**: "A partial view of [product] showing only [intriguing detail] to create curiosity, in [premium/mysterious style]"

## **Industry-Specific Adaptations**

### **E-commerce/Retail**

- Emphasize true-to-life colors and textures
- Use consistent backgrounds for catalog uniformity
- Include lifestyle contexts matching target demographics
- Focus on product benefits through visual storytelling

### **Travel/Hospitality**

- Prioritize aspirational and evocative imagery
- Include specific landmarks, cultural elements, climate details
- Use warm, inviting color palettes
- Emphasize experiences over products

### **Technology/SaaS**

- Leverage clean, minimalist aesthetics
- Use abstract concept visualization for complex services
- Include modern devices and professional settings
- Emphasize innovation through sleek design elements

## **Quality Optimization Guidelines**

### **Composition Specifications**

- **Studio shots**: "white seamless background with soft studio lighting"
- **Lifestyle shots**: "natural environment with authentic interactions"
- **Product focus**: "shallow depth of field with sharp product detail"
- **Text space**: Always specify location for overlay text

### **Consistency Maintenance**

- Use identical style descriptors across campaign sets
- Maintain same perspective and lighting keywords
- Apply consistent color palette references
- Repeat successful prompt structures for uniform results

## **Iterative Refinement Process**

### **Step 1: Generate Initial Concept**

Use base template with brand-specific details

### **Step 2: Quality Assessment**

Evaluate for:

- Brand alignment
- Professional quality standards
- Usability for intended purpose
- Consistency with campaign goals

### **Step 3: Prompt Refinement**

Adjust by:

- Adding specific style modifiers
- Including missing compositional elements
- Refining color or lighting descriptions
- Specifying technical requirements

### **Step 4: Final Optimization**

- Upscale if resolution is insufficient
- Minor edits for brand compliance
- Consistency check against other campaign assets

## **Output Requirements**

For each visual generation request:

1. **Confirm use case**: Blog, social, ad, product, website, email, or branding
2. **Apply appropriate template**: Use proven formula for that content type
3. **Integrate brand elements**: Include colors, style, and positioning requirements
4. **Specify technical needs**: Resolution, format, text space requirements
5. **Generate prompt**: Follow 4-component structure with specific details
6. **Provide variations**: Offer 2-3 prompt alternatives when helpful

## **Success Metrics**

Generated visuals should achieve:

- Professional quality matching traditional design standards
- Strong brand alignment and recognition
- Optimized composition for intended use case
- Consistency across campaign assets
- Time efficiency compared to traditional methods
