# Marketing Angle & Competitor Research Prompt

Type: eCommerce

[PRODUCT NAME + LINK]

Do competitor analysis, reviews, social sentiment, ad psychology, and consumer behavior analysis to deeply understand:

- Why people buy this
- What emotional triggers drive purchases
- What frustrations/pain points it solves
- What identity/status aspirations are tied to it
- What objections customers may have
- What type of people are most likely to impulse buy it
- What type of creatives would make this scale on Meta Ads

Research:

- Competitors
- Existing ads
- Reviews
- TikTok content
- Amazon reviews
- Reddit discussions
- Common complaints
- Common desires
- Market sophistication level
- Saturation level
- Consumer awareness stage
- Emotional language customers naturally use

Then create 5 HIGHLY UNIQUE marketing angles.

I do NOT want generic angles.

I want angles that:

- Feel emotionally intelligent
- Tap into real consumer psychology
- Have strong hooks for Meta ads
- Create curiosity
- Create emotional resonance
- Solve real pain points
- Differentiate from competitors
- Feel native to social media
- Could realistically scale

For EACH angle provide:

1. Angle Name
2. Core Emotional Driver
3. Main Pain Point
4. Target Customer Persona
5. Why this angle could work on Meta
6. Suggested hook variations
7. Suggested opening visual
8. Suggested UGC creator type
9. Suggested ad style:
    - Native UGC
    - Testimonial
    - Problem/solution
    - Founder story
    - Luxury aesthetic
    - Humor
    - Comparison
    - Lifestyle
10. Suggested landing page messaging
11. Most likely objections
12. How to overcome objections
13. Most compelling benefits to emphasize
14. Suggested CTA
15. Estimated scaling potential (1-10)

Then rank the TOP 3 angles most likely to scale profitably on Meta Ads in today’s market.

Finally, provide:

- Which angle you would test first
- What type of creative I should film first
- What type of creator/avatar would work best
- What headline style would likely win
- What emotional tone would likely convert best
- What type of offer would pair best with this product

The goal is to uncover angles that competitors are NOT already heavily using.
