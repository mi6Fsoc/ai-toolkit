# Copywriting Prompts for High‑Converting Ad Copy

### 🔧 How to Use These Prompts

Replace placeholders like [product type], [social media platform], etc., with your specifics for targeted, persuasive ad copy.

## 1. Social Media Ads

1. Product launch
    
    Create a [social media platform] ad for [product type] targeting [demographic or interest group], emphasizing [unique feature or benefit] and encouraging [specific action].
    
2. Seasonal sale
    
    Generate a [social media platform] ad for our [season or holiday]-themed sale, highlighting [type of deal], designed to attract [specific audience] and drive [purchases or store visits].
    
3. Event promotion
    
    Write a [social media platform] ad for [upcoming event], targeting [specific audience], by focusing on [key value of the event].
    
4. Product differentiation
    
    Create a [social media platform] ad promoting [product or service], focusing on [key differentiator], and appealing to [target audience].
    
5. Limited-time offer
    
    Create a [social media platform] ad highlighting our [type of limited-time offers] on [product or service] urging [audience] to act quickly with [call-to-action].
    
6. Awareness campaign
    
    Write a [social media platform] ad to raise awareness about [brand, product, or cause], targeting [specific audience] and emphasizing [key message or value proposition].
    
7. User testimonials
    
    Generate a [social media platform] ad featuring this user testimonial/review for [product or service], targeting [specific audience]: [Add user testimonial/review].
    
8. Upsell or cross-sell
    
    Write a [social media platform] ad to upsell [product or service] to existing customers or cross-sell [related items], by highlighting [additional benefits or complementary features]. Target [specific audience].
    
9. Holiday campaign
    
    Create a [social media platform] ad for our [holiday or occasion]-focused campaign, promoting [special offers or themed products], and targeting [specific audience]. The goal is to drive [engagement or sales].
    
10. Lead generation
    
    Generate a [social media platform] ad for [product or service], targeting [specific audience], with the aim of collecting leads and emphasizing [incentive].

## 5. AI Image Prompts for Ad Creatives

1. Product photo for ad
    
    Generate product image of [product type] placed on a [specify surface type] in a [background setting], captured in a [composition style] for a product advertisement.
    
2. Product comparison for ad
    
    Generate two [product type] photos side by side. The left shows a [generic product type with neutral branding] placed on a [surface type] against a [background setting]. The right features [your product type with special features] on a [type of surface] with a [background setting]. Arrange both in a [composition style] to emphasize their features for an effective product advertisement.
    
3. Product‑in‑use for ad
    
    Generate a product‑in‑use image of [product type] that is being used by [people or person] set against a [background setting]. They are [describe specific action with the product]. The scene should have [mood and lighting details].
    
4. Seasonal or holiday image for ad
    
    Generate a seasonal/holiday‑theme image of [image subject] placed on a [surface type]. Add some [seasonal/holiday decoration elements], and a [background setting] that matches the [season or holiday]. Make sure the photo is arranged in a [composition style] to give it a festive or seasonal feel.
    
5. Thumbnail for video ad
    
    A photo of [image subject] set against a [background setting] in a [composition style] with [lighting and effects] to capture attention.
